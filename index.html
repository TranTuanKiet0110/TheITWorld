• Cloud computing:

Cloud computing is the essential technology that a website or an application nowadays need to have. The reason why cloud computing is very important for any application is that cloud computing works like a data storage (cloud storage). Cloud computing is the delivery of computing services, it includes servers, storage, databases, networking… So why many large companies decided to choose this technology for their applications? Cloud computing have a numerous of advantages. Firstly, using cloud computing can save money because it does not need any physical hardware investments. Using cloud computing, you can direct store data on their servers. Secondly, cloud computing has almost limitless storage capacity. You can expand your cloud storage at any time with a very reasonable monthly fee. Thirdly, the important of cloud computing is back-up and restore data. When you store data on cloud, it is very easy to back-up and recover your data.

Moreover, back-up data by using cloud is faster and cheaper. Last but not least, cloud computing has high speed of data transition, all you just need to do is a few clicks and then you can get the resources required for your system within fewer minutes. Although cloud computing has a lot of advantages, there are some disadvantages within this technology. First of all, the problem that we mostly face is internet connection. You can not access to the cloud if you don’t the internet or if your internet connection is weak. Therefore, you can not upload and store your data on to the cloud. In addition, another drawback of using cloud computing services is the security risk.

Before using cloud computing service, you need to know that you will be sharing all your company’s sensitive information to the third-party cloud computing service provider. Therefore, hacker might access to this information if the provider has a weak security. Thirdly, if the cloud service provider is being cyber-attack, so it will significantly affect the performance of your shared resource. Normally, when using cloud service, you may encounter these cases, for example, server downtime, server maintenance or maybe your may face power loss… Lastly, you may face this problem if your company have a lot of data to store. Cloud computing company just provide a limit bandwidth to their users. So, in case you want to pass the limitation, you will need to charge more money which might be costly.

Cloud computing is causing a major shift for the whole IT industry. This technology allowed us to access to the application through a web browser, which means there is no IT cost for the hardware or servers. Moreover, this change can cause harmful effect to many industries on the world. For example, there are a lot of hardware has been produced but the customer doesn’t need these products anymore. So, it will cause a big loss in money of the industries which they used to invest in their products. In addition, if the loss money is increasing, the industry will be closed.

Cloud computing is not only affecting the IT industry, it also affects the way that the employees work nowadays. When you introduce a new technology to the company, so all the employees need to have a training based on this technology. Because this is a new technology, therefore some people will feed uncomfortable or unfamiliar when working with this technology. Moreover, the need for the IT support staff will be reduced, but the company have to pay for the training costs of the employees. Also, cloud computing can affect our daily life in many different ways. For example, cloud computing can save your money; using cloud computing is cheaper than buying a physical hardware to store more data.

• Cybersecurity and Privacy:

Cybersecurity means defending computers, servers, networks, mobile devices electronic systems from a malicious attack. Cybersecurity is very common nowadays; it is also represented as a software on your computer or devices. For example, anti-virus, anti-spam, …

Cybersecurity and privacy have a lot of benefits and because of these advantages, many companies decided to include this technology into their system. First of all, this technology provides a protection for your business. Cybersecurity will help your employees to get rid of malware and ransomware. Moreover, your business’s data will be protected by this technology, so the chance of hacker break into your business will be reduce. Also, your computer can avoid many harmful viruses to steal or delete your data. By eliminating viruses, it will increase your company’s productivity and the cost for server’s maintenance will be reduce too. Secondly, the most common problem that many companies try to solve is protect their customer’s accounts. For example, the information of a bank account must be kept secret. If your business could avoid this problem, therefore it will inspire customer confidence in using your services and your business’s profit will be increase. Moreover, by gaining trusts from many customers, these customers will introduce your service to other people, so your service become more popular and widely spread to the other countries.

In contrast, there are some drawbacks that usually occur when using cybersecurity technology. In other to use this cybersecurity software and to get the full functions of the software, you need to pay a large amount of money. When using computer build-in software such as windows defender, firewall, it usually gets out of date. Therefore, the software keeps updating in order to keep the protection up to date which normally cause uncomfortable experiences to the users. As the software keep updating, it requires more disk space. This is the reason that make our computers working slowly than before. Moreover, firewall is difficult to configure correctly, so it may prohibit users from performing tasks on the internet. This also make the users feel uncomfortable because of the access denied.

Cybersecurity and privacy are also causing the change in the IT industry. This technology helps many companies to protect their information and their customers information too. If this technology involves in many companies, so less employees will be need for the business security section. But you need to pay money to purchase this technology and to train the employees, so that they can master this new technology. In order to work with this new technology, the level of requirement of the employee will be higher than before, therefore, many employees cannot meet the requirement of the job and they will lose their job. In addition, this technology also helps us in our daily life too. It protects the users from getting to the harmful websites and it keeps users personal information secret. As I think cybersecurity is the best way to keep our personal information safety.
